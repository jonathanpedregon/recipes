# Al Pastor

## Marinade
- 50g pineapple
- 100g orange juice
- 25g water
- 25g white vinegar
- 20g sugar
- 20g salt
- 30g achiote annatto paste
- 2g dried oregano
- 5g garlic powder
- 5g cumin
- 10g chile powder

1. Put all ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth

## Meat
- 1 pork shoulder

1. Cut the meat into small steaks
2. Add the marinade to the meat and let sit for 24 hours
3. Char the meat (broil or grill)
